# Automated Web UI & Accessibility Auditor
### 🚀 Leading the Next Generation of Agentic Accessibility Repairs

## 🌟 The Vision & Problem
Currently, generic AI web-agents are bloated—relying on memory-heavy headless Chromium models that scrape raw HTML blindly. For enterprise-grade accessibility (WCAG) and Semantic SEO auditing, this approach is imprecise, prone to hallucination, and extremely expensive to scale.

Our solution is the **Automated Web UI & Accessibility Auditor**—a zero-browser, strictly-typed Pydantic simulator built efficiently upon the **OpenEnv** framework. We transform the "black box" of web-agent interaction into a deterministic, surgical repair system that handles complex DOM mutations with 100% schema compliance.

## 💡 Technical Innovation
1. **Zero-Browser Footprint (Total Efficiency):**
   We achieve full OpenEnv execution within the restrictive **2 vCPU / 8 GB RAM** limits by completely abandoning Chromium web drivers. The DOM is manipulated purely in memory as a lightweight AST (Abstract Syntax Tree), allowing for blazing-fast inference and massive parallelization.

2. **Strict Pydantic Grounding (No Hallucination):**
   By leveraging the advanced `.parse()` parameters of **OpenEnv** and **pydantic>=2.0**, we eliminate AI hallucination natively. Agents cannot "guess" commands or attributes—they are forced to output strictly valid `update_attribute`, `modify_css`, or `reorder_nodes` actions.

3. **Dense Evaluator Shaping (Not Pass/Fail):**
   Archaic binary "Pass/Fail" metrics don't measure the *nuance* of UI quality. Our environment utilizes **Dense Continuous Rewards (0.0 -> 1.0)**. Fixing accessibility contrast or structural hierarchy yields granular feedback, training agents to understand the *quality* of a fix, not just the presence of one.

4. **Rich Dribbble Aesthetics:**
   The environment isn't just functional; it's **premium**. Inspired by high-end dashboard designs, we use a sophisticated color palette—**Emerald Green (#50C878)** and **Soft Gold (#FFD700)**—to guide the agent's visual optimization tasks.

## 🏗️ 3-Layer Architecture (Anti-Gravity Framework)
- **Directive Layer:** Establishes the task curriculum (Easy: Alt Text Injection, Medium: WCAG Contrast Fixation, Hard: Semantic DOM Sequencing).
- **Orchestration Layer:** Processes agent reasoning against the ideal DOM state.
- **Execution Layer:** Mutates state natively inside the simulator, providing deterministic grading.

## 🚀 Impact & Future
This project proves that **Agentic AI doesn't need a browser to understand the Web.** By simulating the DOM as a strict data structure, we can automate accessibility compliance for millions of pages at a fraction of the current cost/latency.
